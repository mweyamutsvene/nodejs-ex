Innovation Foundry

Mission Statement

Innovation Foundry's purpose is to provide software development experience and education for people living in our community. We provide the means and more importantly, the mentorship to help our students get a leg up in the technology sector, which will prepare them for any career available to them.

Learn more

Vision Statement

Our purpose is to make learning how to program less intimidating. To help individuals realize that they have potential in this field. We want people to realize that programming is not just for extraordinary individuals, similar to what we see in the media, but is a skill that anyone who works hard can become proficient in.

About Us

Innovation Foundry is a registered 503(c) non profit entity registered in the state of California. It is founded by our CEO, Tommy Hanks who has extensive background in mobile application development and mentoring. He of course is supported by his beautiful wife and lovely daughters in all his endeavours.
